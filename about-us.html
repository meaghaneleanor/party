Our Story

StudyPoint's founders had a simple idea: to create a better alternative to impersonal learning centers.

With that goal in mind, co-founders Richard Enos and Gregory Zumas began StudyPoint (StudySmart, Inc.) in Boston in 1999. They focused on the needs of families who wanted a higher level of customer service and convenience, and on students who would benefit from individualized, one-to-one instruction.

Early on, the company invested heavily in curriculum development and training standards, so that its tutors could deliver consistent and reliable academic and test prep results to families. As the company grew it added additional services and programs, ranging from individualized needs assessments to online homework tools.

After establishing a strong base in Boston, the founders repeated their model for success in Washington DC, New York, Chicago, and

Philadelphia. In 2006, the company In 2006, the company added offices in San Francisco and Los Angeles. Over the next two years, StudyPoint opened new offices in eleven different locations: Atlanta, Dallas-Ft.Worth, Detroit, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Columbus, Denver, Detroit, Houston, Minneapolis-St. Paul, and Seattle. By the end of 2009, the company had expanded again to begin working with students in Austin and San Diego.

In 2012, StudyPoint was named to the INC 5,000 list of the fastest growing private companies in America for the sixth consecutive year. But the true measure of StudyPoint's success is its satisfied customers. Since inception, the company and its experienced tutors have helped over fifteen thousand students nationwide increase their confidence, while achieving higher grades and test scores.

Career Opportunities at StudyPoint

StudyPoint is a rapidly growing educational company with an inspiring mission: to build a great company and to help kids succeed in school.

We provide tutoring programs for families and schools, and we have grown on the hard work, intelligence, and dedication of our team. Over the past ten years we have expanded our programs to twenty-one major US cities, and we continue to plan our entry into new markets.

Our philosophy is one of learning and growth, and it applies to both our students and our employees. Helping students and families achieve their educational goals is an incredible experience, and offers a unique combination of rewarding work, constant challenge, and unlimited growth potential.
